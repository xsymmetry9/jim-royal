About Jim Royal

The Jim Royal Trading Company was founded in April 1987 and specializes in the import and export of pharmaceutical active ingredients, fine chemicals, intermediates, veterinary raw materials, feed additives, nutritional ingredients, food additives, and nutraceutical raw materials. The company operated using both stock sales and indent business models, and had partnerships with numerous international manufacturers.
